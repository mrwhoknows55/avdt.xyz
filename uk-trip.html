Mumbai to Delhi Travel Itinerary

Day 1 - Friday, January 31
  • 11:00 Depart Mumbai by Train 12471 SWARAJ EXPRESS to Delhi (NDLS)
Day 2 - Saturday, February 1
  • 5:30 Arrive in Delhi
  • 7:00 Bus from ISBT Delhi to Rishikesh
  • 12:00 Arrive in Rishikesh
  • Evening Explore Rishikesh
  • Night Stay in Rishikesh
Things to Explore in Rishikesh:
  • Attend the famous Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat
  • Visit the Beatles Ashram
  • Explore Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula
  • Try adventure sports (rafting, if time permits)
  • Visit Parmarth Niketan
Day 3 - Sunday, February 2
  • 5:30 Bus from Rishikesh to Joshimath
  • 17:30 Arrive in Joshimath
  • Night Stay in Joshimath
Things to Explore in Joshimath:
  • Visit Narsingh Temple
  • Explore Shankaracharya Math
  • Visit Jyotirmath
  • Local market exploration
  • View of Nanda Devi peak
Day 4 - Monday, February 3
  • 5:30 Depart Joshimath
  • 6:30 Take Ropeway/Cab to Auli
  • Day Explore Auli
  • Night Stay in Auli
Things to Explore in Auli:
  • Skiing (if winter season)
  • Take the chair lift ride
  • Visit Artificial Lake
  • Enjoy panoramic views of Nanda Devi, Mana Parvat and Kamat
  • Gurso Bugyal trek
  • Photography at sunset point
Day 5 - Tuesday, February 4
  • 6:00-13:00 Explore Auli and Joshimath
  • 13:00-14:00 Ropeway/Cab from Auli to Joshimath
  • Night Stay in Joshimath
Day 6 - Wednesday, February 5
  • 6:00 Bus from Joshimath to Haridwar
  • 17:00 Arrive in Haridwar
  • Night Stay in Haridwar
Things to Explore in Haridwar:
  • Attend Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri
  • Visit Mansa Devi Temple (take ropeway)
  • Explore Chandi Devi Temple
  • Visit Daksha Mahadev Temple
  • Shop at Local Markets
Day 7 - Thursday, February 6
  • Until 11:30 Explore Haridwar
  • 11:30 Bus to Delhi
  • 17:30 Arrive in Delhi
  • Night Stay in Delhi
Day 8-9 - Friday-Saturday, February 7-8
  • Full day Delhi Exploration
  • 11:50 (Sat) Flight 6E6047 from Delhi to Mumbai
  • 14:10 (Sat) Arrive in Mumbai
Things to Explore in Delhi:
  • Day 1:
    • Red Fort
    • Jama Masjid
    • Chandni Chowk
    • Qutub Minar
    • Humayun's Tomb
  • Day 2:
    • India Gate
    • Lotus Temple
    • Akshardham Temple
    • Connaught Place
    • Shopping at Sarojini Nagar/Lajpat Nagar
