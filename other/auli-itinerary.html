Important Tips and Notices
  • Aarati Experience: Choose to attend either the Ganga Aarti at Haridwar (Har Ki Pauri) or in Rishikesh (Triveni Ghat or Parmarth Niketan). Both offer a unique and spiritual atmosphere.
  • Weather Preparedness: February in Auli can be extremely cold with snow, so pack heavy winter jackets, thermals, and gloves. Rishikesh is cooler in the evenings, so carry a light jacket.
  • Footwear: Carry waterproof boots with good grip for Auli and comfortable walking shoes for Rishikesh.
  • Road Conditions: Roads to Auli can be icy; confirm the weather and road status before traveling.
  • Activities: Book skiing or cable car activities in Auli in advance. Try river rafting in Rishikesh if weather permits.
  • Health Precautions: Keep motion sickness medicine for mountain roads, basic first aid, cold and fever medicine, and any personal prescriptions.
  • Connectivity: Mobile signals may be patchy in Auli; download offline maps and carry cash as ATMs may be limited.
  • Hotel Stays: Book hotels in advance, especially in Auli, as it’s a popular spot. Ensure your hotel has heating facilities and running hot water.
  • Essentials: Pack sunscreen, sunglasses, lip balm, and a reusable water bottle for both locations.
  • Local Cuisine: Try local Garhwali dishes and enjoy riverside cafes in Rishikesh for a unique experience.
